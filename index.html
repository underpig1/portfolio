MOBILE APP   •   WEBSITE   •   COMMUNITY

GiveBack Hawaii

CLIMATE ACTION FROM HOME

The climate crisis requires immediate and collective action from everyone. Residents of Hawaii can download the GBHI app on their mobile device and fight the crisis from home. The app motivates behavioral changes by "gameifying" the process of reducing carbon emissions and allowing users to connect over legislative climate reform campaigns and environmental events like beach and river cleanups. 2nd place winner of the district 2023 Congressional App Challenge.
