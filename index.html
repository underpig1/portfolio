GiveBack Hawaii

CLIMATE ACTION FROM HOME

Frustrated with the lack of attention the issue was receiving, I developed this mobile app to urge climate action in my home state of Hawaii for the 2022 Congressional App Challenge. Users can contribute to the fight against climate change in a variety of ways with the app: they can participate in community campaigns to reduce their energy consumption, join public events, and learn more about ways they can minimize their carbon emissions. When users reduce their home's energy consumption or complete a campaign, they are awarded points that can be redeemed for fun climate-related prizes like recyclable water bottles, funded by in-app advertisements. I received a Certificate of Special Congressional Recognition for creating it and won 2nd place in my district's Congressional App Challenge.

RESEARCH   •   MACHINE LEARNING   •   GENETICS

GENN Rice Predictor

MACHINE LEARNING FOR GENETICS

GENN Rice is machine learning software I developed as part of a team working on a summer research project under a competitive grant program. Our goal was to use machine learning to predict an organism's expressed traits from its genetic makeup. GENN is a set of three machine learning models coded in Python, trained to predict 12 quantitative agronomic traits from DNA strands in rice samples. The goal of GENN was to develop a novel method to reduce costs and improve accuracy when performing genome-wide association studies. The details of GENN are included in a research paper pending publication.

Octos Engine

DESKTOP CUSTOMIZATION TOOLS

I created Octos to solve a very specific problem I had: my desktop wallpaper was boring. Octos Engine allows users to customize their desktop background with animated, interactive wallpapers and informative widgets, like stock market performance, the current date and time, and reminders. Octos provides users with an API to quickly develop their own personal desktop as well, either by coding one with HTML or by using the desktop editor. Users can then share their creations to the module library, where other users from around the world can download their creations. This software has received notable recognition, has over 500 beta version downloads, and is set to be available for public release in the near future.

VIDEO GAME   •   MULTIPLAYER   •   PHYSICS

Harpy

TAKE TO THE SKIES

My goal for this project was originally to experiment and play around with terrain generation algorithms, but let's be honest, what's the point if I can't explore it from a little cartoon biplane? After that thought, my adventure into procedural mesh generation evolved into creating a fully functional 3D multiplayer flight simulator. In Free Flight, players can explore an infinite chunk-based procedural terrain that features a handful of different biomes, including beaches, tropical islands, hills, grasslands, and snow-capped mountain ranges. In Dogfight, players can compete against each other in playful aircraft battles through a local Wi-Fi connection in one of four enclosed arenas.

MOBILE APP   •   WORD GAME   •   MULTIPLAYER

Vocabulum

LETTER BY LETTER

I've always loved word games, so it's only fitting that I decided to create my own. As a twist on classic word games like Scrabble, Vocabulum is a rapid-fire mobile vocab game that requires speed, agility, and word knowledge. Players can compete against each other online to sharpen their skills and earn rewards, or they can just play solo against the clock. Players can participate in a handful of competitive game modes, including classic mode, online versus, masters mode, and daily versus. They can also earn fun rewards to commemorate their victories, including trophies and customization items.
